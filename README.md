# InvestHer: Women's Investment Research & Platform

🎯 Mission

Investing isn't about being fearless. It's about being informed. It's about knowing your why before you know the numbers.

Women Invest Tracker is a premium investment companion designed for women in India who want to build wealth with intention, clarity, and confidence.


✨ What We Built

A data-driven platform + comprehensive case study that tackles a critical market gap:

The Problem


Women control 47% of investable assets in India
Yet represent only 23% of active investors
Face barriers: knowledge gaps, risk aversion, lack of relatable guidance
Existing platforms weren't built for women — they were built for everyone


The Solution

Women Invest Tracker: Track. Learn. Grow.


📊 Case Study Inside

This repo includes a comprehensive research document analyzing:

Research Methodology


Sample: 22 female respondents across India
Method: K-Means behavioral segmentation, financial literacy mapping
Scope: Investment patterns, decision drivers, barriers & opportunities


Key Findings

✅ 4 Distinct Investor Personas identified (Conservative, Growth-Oriented, Curiosity-Driven, Impact-Focused)

✅ Primary Barriers: Risk aversion (34%), Knowledge gaps (29%), Time constraints (21%)

✅ Market Opportunity: TAM/SAM/SOM modeled across income & geography

✅ Unit Economics: 3-scenario DCF projections (Conservative/Base/Aggressive)

✅ Actionable Insights: Platform features prioritized by persona preferences
